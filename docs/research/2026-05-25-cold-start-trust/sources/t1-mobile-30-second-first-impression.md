## Key Findings

1. **Average mobile app loses 77% of users in the first 3 days post-install.** Most attrition happens fast; the first-session experience is heavily load-bearing.
2. **D1 retention averages ~25%, D30 averages ~5.7% across 31 categories.** Fintech, social, and productivity over-index; gaming and ecommerce under-index.
3. **Apps that activate users within 3 minutes see ~2× higher retention.** "Time-to-first-value" is the operational metric, and the threshold is fast.
4. **Speed and responsiveness signal trust.** Even 100ms latency penalties measurably hurt D1 retention. Performance is a trust dimension, not just a UX dimension.
