## Key Findings

1. **The biggest cause of onboarding drop-offs is not poor UI, but lack of trust.** For sensitive-data products especially (fintech, healthtech), trust is the gating concern.
2. **Clear, honest microcopy reduces drop-off, especially for fintech and healthtech.** Naming what the product does, what it doesn't do, and why — directly at the trust-blocking moment.
3. **First-time delivery of value is the most important trust event.** All abstract trust signals fall away if the first interaction does what it promised.
4. **A third of users cancel before the first month is up; many decide in the first week.** Onboarding's trust window is short.
