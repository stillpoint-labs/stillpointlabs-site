## Key Findings

1. **Day 1 is about first-session quality; Day 7 is about habit formation; Day 30 is about durable PMF.** Each milestone marks a trust threshold the user crosses.
2. **Average D30 retention is 5-7% across categories.** Fintech sits at 10-15%, social at 15-20%, productivity at 10-18%. Stillpoint's category (household-utility-with-financial-or-meal-stakes) doesn't have a clean benchmark; the closest is fintech (10-15% target) and productivity (10-18%).
3. **D90 flatness signals durable PMF.** Consumer apps that flatten at 5-15% have PMF; SaaS that flattens at 40-60% has it. The curve SHAPE matters more than any single number.
4. **A third of users cancel before the first month is up; many make the decision in the first week.** The 30-day window is the operative trust-formation period.
