## Key Findings

- Woebot has 18 trials and is the most research-grounded mental-health chatbot in the consumer category.
- RCT evidence shows efficacy at reducing depression/anxiety symptoms in 2-week trials.
- Despite the evidence base, the D2C product was shut down June 2025; company pivoted to enterprise (payer/provider).
- **The single most important data point for this research:** research-grounded does NOT automatically equal commercially defensible. Evidence base produces clinical credibility; clinical credibility produces enterprise/B2B sustainability; consumer market punishes lack of engagement-bait even when evidence is present.
