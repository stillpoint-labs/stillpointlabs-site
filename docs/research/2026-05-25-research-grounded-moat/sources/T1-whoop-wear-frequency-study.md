## Key Findings

- N=11,914 subscribers, ~1M person-days observed; wear-frequency correlated with HRV, RHR, sleep consistency improvements over 12 weeks.
- WHOOP publishes its research; the company is identifiable as "research-grounded in practice" rather than "research-grounded in marketing."
- Separate WHOOP study (N=38,838) on circadian-alignment behaviors found significant improvements in sleep consistency, RHR, and HRV.
- The pattern: research-grounding shows up in *which behaviors WHOOP measures* (circadian, recovery, sleep architecture) not in *whether WHOOP uses behavioral psychology in its UI*. Different from Stillpoint's claim.
