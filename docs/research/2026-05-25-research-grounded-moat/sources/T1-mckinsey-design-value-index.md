## Key Findings

- McKinsey 2018 study: 300 companies, 5 years; higher-MDI companies saw up to 56% higher returns to shareholders.
- The MDI's four design themes: analytical leadership, cross-functional talent, continuous iteration, user experience.
- "Continuous iteration" and "user experience" are the research-relevant themes; "analytical leadership" maps to research-grounded decision-making.
- **The institutional consensus is clear:** design-led companies (which include research-grounded ones) outperform peers. Stillpoint's thesis sits inside this consensus.
- The unresolved question — whether *research-grounded* specifically beats *opinionated-design-led* — is NOT answered by McKinsey. Both are inside the "design-led" bucket.
