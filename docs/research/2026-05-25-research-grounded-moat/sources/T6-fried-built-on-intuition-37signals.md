## Key Findings

- The 37signals position: intuition + opinionated product + real-product feedback beats upfront research, *for their category* (B2B productivity, smaller team, experienced founders).
- Shape Up methodology (Singer 2019) explicitly skips upfront research in favor of 6-week shaped bets and post-ship learning.
- Key qualifier: their stack works because (a) founders have 20+ years of relevant experience, (b) they ship to a category they know intimately, (c) their business model (paid subscription, retention-stable) doesn't require engagement-tricks.
- Failure mode they don't discuss: when intuition is wrong because the audience is unlike the founders. Their products fit their audience because their audience is them. That's not a generalizable strategy.
