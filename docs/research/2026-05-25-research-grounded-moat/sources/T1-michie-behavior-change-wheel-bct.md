## Key Findings

- Michie's BCT Taxonomy v1 is the canonical operationalization of "research-grounded UX" in the health behavior-change literature.
- 93 behavior-change techniques (BCTs), 16 groupings, peer-reviewed and field-adopted.
- Used in app-design contexts across diabetes, smoking cessation, physical activity, mental health.
- The taxonomy is freely available; the moat is not the taxonomy itself but *which BCTs a product implements and how*.
- Implication for Stillpoint: the deep-research skill is the closest internal analog. Productization candidates are downstream — methodologies for translating BCTs into specific UX decisions per discipline (physiology, psychology, marital-dynamics).
