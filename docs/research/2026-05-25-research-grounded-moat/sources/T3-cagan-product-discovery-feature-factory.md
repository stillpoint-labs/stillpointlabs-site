## Key Findings

- Cagan: discovery is the load-bearing skill; "feature factory" produces ~15% of shipped features that move a metric.
- This is the consensus product-management view; discovery is the modal framing in 2024-2026.
- **The critical finding for RQ3:** even with Cagan-style discovery being the consensus *talk*, most teams do not actually practice it. The feature factory is the modal *behavior*. The gap between stated discovery practice and actual discovery practice is the moat.
- Cagan's "discovery" is closer to lightweight user-research interviews + prototype testing. Stillpoint's deep-research is deeper (peer-reviewed lit integration, source-evaluation rubric, 10-dim credibility scoring). Stillpoint is at the more-rigorous end of the discovery continuum.
