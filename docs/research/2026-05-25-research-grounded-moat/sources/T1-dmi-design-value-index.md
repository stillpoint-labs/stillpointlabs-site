## Verified Quote(s)

> "Design-led companies outperformed the S&P 500 index by 228% over ten years." — DMI Design Value Index page, paragraph 1. Access: live.

## Key Findings

- 15-16 "design-driven" exemplars (Apple, IBM, Nike, P&G, Starbucks et al.) returned 228% over the S&P 500 over a ten-year window.
- McKinsey Design Index found higher-design-scoring companies returned up to 56% more to shareholders over 5 years.
- The Finnish Design Value Index replicates the direction in a different geography.
- The causal mechanism is not isolated — design-led companies may also be better-managed companies in general (the headline confounder for this study).
