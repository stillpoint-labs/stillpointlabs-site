## Key Findings

- Gottman Method: 40 years longitudinal observation; four divorce-predictors (criticism, defensiveness, contempt, stonewalling) replicate.
- EFT: attachment-theory-grounded; multiple RCTs; 70-75% distress-to-recovery, 90% significant improvement.
- Both are empirically supported couples therapy approaches per APA criteria.
- The research base for marital/family-dynamics is *more rigorous* than the research base for physiology in mental-health apps (polyvagal contested) or behavioral economics in consumer SaaS (nudge replication crisis).
- **Implication for Stillpoint:** the marital/family-dynamics layer is the *strongest* of the three research layers in the blend. If anything, Stillpoint is under-citing this literature relative to its empirical strength.
